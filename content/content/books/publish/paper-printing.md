## Common services
- Regular small printer - Dye ink (water soluble)
- Medium printer - Pigment ink (water-proof - the best)
- Large printer - 
  - Offset ink (almost water-proof). Min 100 copies.
    - laser, with plates
  - Digital printer - Powder ink (fades away)
